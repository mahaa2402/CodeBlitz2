About Our System

Our system harnesses the power of advanced computer vision to detect and categorize a variety of road obstacles:

  • Pedestrians: Ensuring pedestrian safety with accurate detection.
  • Vehicles (cars, trucks, buses): Monitoring vehicular traffic for potential hazards.
  • Cyclists: Identifying cyclists to prevent accidents and promote safety.
  • Animals: Detecting animals on the road to minimize collisions.
  • Traffic signals and signs: Ensuring compliance and safety through signal detection.

We leverage sophisticated algorithms to classify and prioritize objects, enhancing overall road safety.
